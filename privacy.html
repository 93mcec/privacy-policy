WoT Stats Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how WoT Stats handles user information.

Data Collection

WoT Stats does not collect, store, sell, or share personal user data.

The application retrieves publicly available World of Tanks statistics through official APIs and does not require user registration.

Third-Party Services

The application may access publicly available data provided by official World of Tanks services.

Advertising

WoT Stats does not display advertisements.

Children's Privacy

The application is not directed specifically at children and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
